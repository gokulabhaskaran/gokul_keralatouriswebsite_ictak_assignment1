Alappuzha

Alappuzha - Venice of The East

The district of Alappuzha is a widely known tourist destination and is well known for its coir factories. Alappuzha is strongly connected by waterways to various other parts of Kerala, including the famous tourist destination, Kumarakom. The most important tourist centres of state is also known as Venice of the East and ideal headquarters for backwater tourism. The Backwaters of Alappuzha are the most popular tourist attraction in Kerala,houseboat cruise,ambalappuzha payasam and Alappuzha Beach are other attractions in Alappuzha and offers the most beautiful views of the Laccadive Sea.
